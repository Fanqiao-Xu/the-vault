Aalto House — Helsinki, 2024

On
Spatial
Moats

A spatial moat is the kind of brand asset algorithms can observe but never replicate — the capacity of a physical space to produce presence, slowness, and genuine decision in a visitor. Aalto designed it into a tuberculosis sanatorium. Aesop built it into a cosmetics store. The brands that understand the difference between décor and mechanism are building something that doesn't erode.
